High Quality Services

JIL Medical Diagnostics and Laboratories is a private owned laboratory aimed at recalculatively carrying out effective and affordable tests for the wellbeing of patients both in Uyo and other parts of the country. We have a well equipped Chemical Pathology, Blood bank and other medical segments to better take care of our clients to a satisfactory level.

Chemical Pathology

Chemical Pathology department comprises of tests like EUC, Fasting Blood Sugar, HBAIC, Hormonal assays, Thyroid Function Tests(TFT), Liver Function Test(LFT), Urinalysis, C-Reactive Protein(CRP), Electrolytes. Random Blood Sugar (RBC.)
